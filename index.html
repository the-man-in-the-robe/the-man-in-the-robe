Why trust a chief judge
Who asks to see not.

What justice exists
Where fact is not sought.

Who?

So blind is a court
Too easy boycott.

That hides away from
The truth of a plot.

Who?

We've become so frail
We hide from a thought.

How is a witness
The Nation's weak spot?

Who?

Lost is the lady
For whom many have fought?

A great sacrafice
Succumbs to the rot.

Who?

How will our children's
History be taught.

The man in the robe
Demands what he ought.

Or will it soon end
That he would ask not.
