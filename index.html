to the Man in the Robe

 

What justice exists
Where fact is unsought.

Why trust in a chief
Who asks to see not.

Who?

So, blind is a court
Too easy boycott,

That hides away from
The truth of a plot.

Who?

We've become frail with
Unrigorous thought.

How is a witness
The Nation's weak spot?

Who?

Lost is the lady
For whom many have fought?

Liberty's branches
Succumb to the rot.

Who?

How will our children's
History be taught.

The man in the robe
Demands what he ought.

Or will it soon end
That he would ask not.
